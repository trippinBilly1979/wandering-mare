The Wandering Mare

The Wandering Mare is a unique mobile bar in the greater Peoria/Bloomington areas. We offer both wet and dry bartending services for any event, but can also be used for display and or photo prop purposes. No matter the event, large or small, The Wandering Mare is sure to take your party to a whole new leve

Following a brief consultation to learn about your event, we can discuss drink packages and create a custom drink from fresh ingredients. We will then provide you with a detailed drink ingredient shopping list, eliminating any guess work on your part.

Wandering Mare comes with all the tools necessary to service your wedding, party, or special event and all bartenders are BAC certified.

What we offer: · 1 BAC certified bartender per 50 guests · Plug in electivity or gas generator access if needed · Set up/breakdown of bar · Service staff · Travel up to 25 miles is free · The “Basic Mixer” package when event plans to serve alchohol · General liability insurance · Set up, tear down, and maintenance of bar area · Garnish prep and upkeep · A detailed alcohol and cocktail ingredient ordering guide · Bartender tool kit (some utensils may not be required for every event): o Bar mat o Shaker o Strainer o Jigger set o Muddler o Wine opener o Bar spoon o Ice bucket o Ice scoop o Garnish tray
